The brief history of TRIGGER. The most rebellious anime studio

Studio Trigger is a Japanese animation studio known for it's rebellious works and lack of worry towards new trends. They have pioneered new avenues of funding using the crowd funding site Kickstarter to fund their film Little Witch Academia: The Enchanted Parade. With their smash television hit Kill La Kill in the fall 2014 to winter 2015 anime season, TRIGGER has established themselves as one of the greatest cult anime studios of the modern anime era.
